The Rules:

This game functions somewhat similar to battleship.
You must click the spaces on the board that represent enemy ships.
Ship spaces will display a fire picture when clicked, and non-ship spaces
will display a white flag when clicked.

Below, you can change the difficulty of the board you want to play on.

Easy: 2 ships, both size 5, 1 vertical ship and 1 horizontal ship. 25 turns.

Intermediate: 3 ships, 2 vertical and 1 horizontal, size 2, 3, 4. 25 turns.

Hard: 4 ships, 2 vertical and 2 horizontal, size 2, 3, 4, 5. 25 turns.

Once you have selected a difficulty, press the start button to begin.
